1 · Overview

Earth Breeze, Inc. is a rapidly growing direct-to-consumer eco-friendly laundry care brand. We ship approximately 2.9 million residential parcels per year across a two-origin network — stable at 200–240k parcels/month — with a distinctive lightweight profile: 64% of parcels under one pound, 84% under two. We seek a carrier partner, or a complementary multi-carrier mix, delivering reliable 2–5 day residential service at highly competitive lightweight rates.

  • Origins: Harrodsburg, KY 40330 (East) and White City, OR 97503 (West, Earth Breeze-operated). Carriers may bid either origin or both; separate rate cards per origin.
  • Portability: Kentucky volume migrated from presort mail (90% of January volume) to ground carriers within one quarter — network volume moves to whoever wins on cost and service. Earth Breeze retains routing flexibility between origins.
  • Sustainability: carbon-neutral or reduced-emission programs, methodology, and emissions reporting are weighted in evaluation.

3 · Scope & requirements

Core service

  • Economy residential ground, 2–5 day, 4 oz to 20 lb (e.g., USPS Ground Advantage, FedEx Ground Economy, UPS Ground Saver, DHL eCommerce, or equivalent).
  • A lightweight product for the sub-1 lb envelope (64% of network volume; the Oregon origin ships a median 0.36 lb parcel) — quote mail-class, presort, or lightweight parcel products explicitly alongside ounce tiers.
  • Sharp 1–3 lb pricing (27% of volume, concentrated at Kentucky, trending heavier).
  • Standard ground and limited 2–3 day expedited as ancillary (<1% of volume).

Operational

  • Daily scheduled pickup at each origin served (Mon–Fri minimum; state Saturday availability).
  • Peak network capacity ≥ 20,000 parcels/day without embargo; state per-origin capacity, volume caps, GRI timing, and peak surcharge calendars.
  • Label generation via API and/or major multi-carrier platforms; EDI 210/214 or API equivalents; full tracking to final delivery including postal handoff.

Service levels

  • ≥95% on-time vs published TNT (monthly, by zone) · damage/loss ≤0.3% · claims resolved ≤30 days · tracking latency ≤4h · invoice accuracy ≥98% · quarterly business reviews.
  • Current KY baseline to meet or beat (median transit): Amazon Ground 3.6d · Veho 3.8d · USPS GA 3.8d · FedEx GE 4.6d · OSM 5.0d.

4 · Pricing format

  • Editable Excel rate cards, separate per origin bid (zones from 40330 and/or 97503), with ounce tiers at minimum 4 / 8 / 12 / 15.999 oz, then per-pound to 20 lb.
  • All accessorials unbundled: residential, DAS/EDAS (with ZIP list), fuel basis + current %, peak/demand surcharges with 2026–27 calendar, additional handling, address correction, RTS.
  • Dimensional divisor and dim treatment of flat polybag mailers; any minimum billable weight.
  • Payment terms, invoice cadence, audit/dispute process, rebates; rate validity, GRI mechanics. Multi-year with capped increases encouraged.
  • Postal-workshare or consolidation models: state induction points, transit impact, liability.
